Privacy Policy

DryFire is built with your privacy as a top priority. We believe your training data should stay with you, always.

  • Microphone Access: Required only for audio detection during timing sessions. All audio is processed locally on your device and never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.
  • No Account Required: Train immediately without creating accounts, providing personal information, or logging in.
  • Zero Analytics: No third-party tracking, analytics tools, or data collection of any kind.
  • Completely Offline: All features work without internet connectivity. Your training data never leaves your device.
  • No Subscriptions: One-time purchase with no recurring fees, subscription tracking, or payment monitoring.

Questions about our privacy practices? Contact us using the support email above.
