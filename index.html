Privacy Policy

DryFire respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • Microphone Access: Required to detect dry-fire audio for timing. Audio is processed locally and never recorded or transmitted.
  • No Account: The app does not require login or personal identification.
  • No Analytics: The app does not use third-party tracking or analytics tools.
  • Offline Use: All features work without internet connectivity.

For any questions regarding privacy, please contact us via the support email listed above.
